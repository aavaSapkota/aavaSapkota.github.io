About Me!

Hi, I'm Aava Sapkota! I'm a currently a student in the MaCS program at William Lyon Mackenzie CI. I have a strong interest in Computer Science and the world of technology. I am an extremely enthusiastic and creative person who loves to learn new things, and would make a great edition to any team!

Currently I am a Black belt in Taekwondo and continue to attend classes. I am also a Provincial Deca competitor and am a proud member of my school's DECA 2020-2021 exec team. I placed 1st in poomsae and 2nd in sparring at my Taekwondo school's last tournament, and was 3rd in my judge at the Deca provincials event this year. In my spare time I enjoy free writing, reading, playing piano (as well as the occasional Netflix binge).

When I look to the future, I often see a career in a Technologies industry. I got my first taste of the real world of tech when I attended my first Hackathon this year at Waterloo for Hack the North. I met amazing people and gained a perspective I never would have otherwise.
